TechVeda is your pocket programming library — built for students who learn best with clear guides and quick help when they get stuck.

📖 READ GUIDES IN THE APP
Open curated PDF courses inside TechVeda. Search and filter by category. No need to hunt files across folders — everything is organized in one modern home screen.

Tap “Ask AI” for friendly explanations on concepts from your courses. TechVeda AI is built by Suresh Yadav to support learners in this app — your study companion, not a generic chatbot.
