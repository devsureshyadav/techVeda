Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

Welcome to TechVeda! Your privacy and trust are extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy details how the TechVeda mobile application and this website collect, process, and protect your information.

TechVeda is developed by Suresh Yadav as a completely free, educational platform to empower students with clean, distraction-free programming guides, interactive offline course PDFs, and Gemini-powered AI tutoring.

1. Information We Collect

Because TechVeda is built from the ground up as a privacy-first educational tool, we strive to minimize data collection:

  • No Account Required: You do not need to register, create an account, or log in to use the TechVeda mobile app or website. Your learning experience is completely anonymous.
  • Offline Course Reading: The in-app PDF course reader runs fully locally on your device. We do not track, collect, or store which manuals or pages you read.
  • AI Tutoring Prompts: When you use the Gemini AI Study Assistant in the app or the simulator on our website, the prompts you type are securely sent directly to Google Gemini API endpoints to generate custom explanations. These prompts are not stored on any separate databases or sold to third parties.
  • Device and Usage Information: We may collect generic, non-identifying technical statistics (such as OS version, device model, and active features utilized) to diagnose crashes, optimize memory usage, and enhance the interface.

2. Third-Party Services & Advertising

To support the cost of providing free study tools and maintaining the Google Gemini API integration, TechVeda utilizes highly optimized third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We serve distraction-free banner and occasional transition ads using AdMob. AdMob may utilize unique device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) and approximate location details to compile anonymous user insights and provide relevant advertisements.
  • Google Gemini API: The AI tutoring components are powered by Google's Gemini models. Your inputs to the study assistant are processed in accordance with Google's API Privacy Policies.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

On this landing and simulation website, we do not utilize trackers, analytic pixels, or persistent database cookies. We may use temporary browser session storage or local storage exclusively to preserve the active state of your interactive chat simulator demo so you can resume your questions seamlessly.

4. Data Security

We employ robust security practices to safeguard all processed data. Communication with the Google Gemini API is encrypted via secure HTTPS protocol. Since the core application stores your course resources locally, your learning history remains safely isolated on your physical device.

5. Children's Privacy

TechVeda is designed specifically for students and developers learning computer science. We do not knowingly collect or request personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe any such data has accidentally been processed, please reach out so we can immediately purge it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to align with new features, operating system requirements, or privacy practices. Any revisions will be marked with a new "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
